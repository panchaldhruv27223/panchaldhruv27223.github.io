About Me

I am a passionate Machine Learning researcher and practitioner currently pursuing my M.Tech in Machine Learning at Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology (DA-IICT). My academic journey is distinguished by excellence, having been awarded the Gold Medal for my Bachelor's degree from Gandhinagar Institute of Technology. I specialize in developing cutting-edge solutions at the intersection of computer vision, multimodal AI, and quantum machine learning.

My research portfolio showcases innovative work in satellite image analysis, where I developed a novel hybrid pipeline combining YOLOS and SAM architectures, achieving a 23% improvement in IoU over baseline models. I've engineered custom vision-language models from scratch, integrating SigLIP vision transformers with Gemma decoders for image-conditioned text generation. Additionally, I've explored quantum machine learning, developing hybrid quantum neural networks that achieve 81.12% accuracy on CIFAR-10, demonstrating my versatility across emerging AI paradigms.

Beyond my research endeavors, I am deeply committed to knowledge dissemination and mentoring. As a Teaching Assistant at DA-IICT, I guide over 350 students through complex machine learning concepts, fostering the next generation of AI practitioners. My passion for sharing knowledge extends to technical writing, with detailed blog posts on Medium covering topics from scikit-learn mastery to advanced TensorBoard visualization. I am driven by the challenge of translating cutting-edge research into practical solutions that can make a real-world impact.
